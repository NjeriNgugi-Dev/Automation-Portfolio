### 1. GitHub Developer Finder

### 2. Web Scraping - Real Estate Agencies (Kenya)
**Purpose:** Lead generation for real estate industry outreach  
**What it does:** Collects agency names, contact info and listings data  
**Output:** Database of real estate agencies for marketing campaigns  
**File:** `Web Scraping- Real Estate Agencies KE.json`

### 3. Web Scraping - Law Firms in Kenya
**Purpose:** B2B lead generation for legal industry  
**What it does:** Extracts law firm data including practice areas and contact details  
**Output:** Targeted prospect list for legal services  
**File:** `Web Scraping- Law Firms in KE.json`

### 4. Booking Agent
**Purpose:** Automated appointment scheduling system  
**What it does:** Manages bookings, sends confirmations, handles calendar integration  
**Output:** Streamlined booking process without manual intervention  
**File:** `Booking agent.json`

### 5. E-commerce Chatbot
**Purpose:** 24/7 automated customer service for online stores  
**What it does:** Answers product questions, handles inquiries, provides support  
**Output:** Instant customer responses and reduced support workload  
**File:** `Ecommerce Chatbot.json`
